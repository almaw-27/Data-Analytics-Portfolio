# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- Ahmed handled the highest number of customer support tickets.
- Addis generated the highest ticket volume.
- Network issues were the most common customer problem.
- Billing issues required the longest average resolution time.
- Addis achieved the highest customer satisfaction score.

## Recommendations

1. Increase training for Billing support processes.
2. Continue monitoring Network issues to reduce ticket volume.
3. Balance workloads among agents.
4. Share Ahmed's best practices with the team.
5. Focus on improving customer satisfaction in Dire Dawa.
