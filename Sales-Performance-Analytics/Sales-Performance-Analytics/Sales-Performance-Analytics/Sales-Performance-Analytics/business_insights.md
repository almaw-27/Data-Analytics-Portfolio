# Business Insights

- Laptop generated the highest revenue.
- Electronics contributed the highest sales.
- Addis was the highest-performing region.
- John achieved the highest sales revenue.
- Total sales reached 101,000 ETB.

## Recommendations

1. Increase stock of high-performing products.
2. Expand successful sales strategies to other regions.
